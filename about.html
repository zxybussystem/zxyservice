Our 25-Year Journey

Founded over two decades ago, Zheng Xing Yun (ZXY) Service began with a simple mission: to provide safe, punctual, and comfortable transport for the people of Singapore.

What started as a small fleet has grown into a trusted name in the industry. For 25 years, we have been the backbone of daily commutes for schools, corporations, and community groups.

Operating out of our headquarters at 2 Defu Lane 10, we take pride in our local heritage and our commitment to maintaining the highest safety standards for every passenger.
